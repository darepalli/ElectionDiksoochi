Transparency, Sources and Data Privacy

We do not collect, store, or sell any personal user data. Your answers stay in your browser session and are used only to calculate your on-screen match results.

Policy theses are compiled from publicly available material and each party position should cite a source. You can review the source policy here: Methodology & Source Links. Learn the project goals here: About this Compass.

We welcome correction requests with documentary evidence. This project is an independent civic information tool, not affiliated with any party or government body.
